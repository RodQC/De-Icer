What is De-Icer?

De-Icer is a community safety app that makes it easy to report ICE activity, learn your rights, and findtrusted support. Our goal is to help neighbors protect each other through verified information.

  • Report & map sightings with time, place, and details
  • Quick "Know Your Rights" guides for home, street, and work
  • Directories of legal aid & rapid-response groups
